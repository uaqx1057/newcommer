Founder Story

Dedicated to Newcomer Success

Huma Tahir is the Founder & CEO of Newcomer Connect and a Canadian settlement expert with over 25 years of hands-on experience in social services.

As an immigrant herself, she understands the emotional and practical realities of starting over in a new country. Her leadership ensures every client receives personalized, transparent, and practical support from pre-arrival planning to successful integration.

Leadership Team

Meet Our Experts

Experienced professionals supporting your journey at every stage.

Jon Brock

Housing & Settlement Advisor

Jon Brock is a seasoned real estate professional focused on helping newcomers settle successfully in Canada. He supports individuals and families with renting, buying, and short-term accommodation options, including Airbnb, so they can make informed housing decisions during their transition.

Hussain Bukhari

Legal Counsel – Immigration & Refugee Law

Hussain Bukhari is a Canada-based immigration and refugee lawyer with extensive experience in refugee claims, humanitarian applications, and immigration appeals. As a former Member of the Refugee Protection Division, he brings deep insight into Canada’s refugee determination system and legal framework.

Abby

Director – Business Advisory & Investor Support

Abby leads international seminars and outreach focused on business migration and investment readiness. She supports investors and entrepreneurs with business planning, startup guidance, and understanding the Canadian market so they can make informed decisions.

JD

Sales & Education Advisor

JD guides newcomers on education pathways in Canada, including credential evaluation procedures, school/college/university systems, and student funding options. He helps families make confident education decisions before and after arrival.
