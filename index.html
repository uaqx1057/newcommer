About Us

Established Expertise, Human-Centred Approach

At Newcomer Connect, we provide personalized immigration and settlement support to clients worldwide planning to move to Canada. Founded by Huma Tahir a settlement support expert with 25+ years of experience.

  • One-on-one guidance tailored to your goals and circumstances
