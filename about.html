Our Mission

Making Canada Accessible to Every Newcomer

Newcomer Connect was founded to bridge the gap between aspiration and arrival. We understand the challenges of starting over in a new country because our founder has lived it.

Led by Huma Tahir, a Canadian settlement expert with over 25 years of experience in social services, our team delivers practical, ethical, and people-centred support at every stage.

  • Personalised one-on-one guidance for every client
  • Virtual services available to clients worldwide
  • Physical offices in Toronto, GTA and Islamabad
  • Transparent, ethical, and culturally sensitive approach

Our Founder

Meet Huma Tahir

Founder & CEO

Huma Tahir

With over 25 years of experience in Canadian social services and settlement support, Huma Tahir leads Newcomer Connect with an unwavering commitment to ethical guidance and newcomer success.

As an immigrant herself, she deeply understands the challenges of starting over in a new country. Her leadership is rooted in providing transparent, practical, and people-centred support that helps individuals and families transition with confidence and dignity.

"My goal is simple every family that works with us should arrive, settle, and thrive."
