Comprehensive guidance delivered virtually before you land in Canada so you arrive prepared, confident, and ready to thrive.

What We Cover

Plan Your Move Before You Land

Our pre-arrival programme equips you with everything needed to make a confident, organised arrival in Canada. We work with you virtually, wherever you are in the world.

  • Personalised settlement action plan and timeline
  • Housing research neighbourhoods, costs, and options
  • Budgeting and financial preparation guidance
  • Banking setup types of accounts and requirements
  • Healthcare registration and OHIP planning
  • Job market overview and credential assessment
  • School system and enrollment guidance for families
  • Document checklist and pre-landing preparation
