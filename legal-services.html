Areas of Practice - Immigration & Refugee Law

Comprehensive legal services covering all aspects of Canadian immigration

Express Entry

Complete support for federal and provincial economic immigration pathways. Services include profile creation, CRS score optimization, provincial nomination guidance, and full application management.

Family Sponsorship

Professional assistance with sponsoring spouses, partners, dependent children, and parents. We guide you through eligibility requirements, financial obligations, and relationship documentation.

Study Permits & Academic Pathways

Strategic guidance for students planning to study in Canada. Services include school selection support, application preparation, and study permit processing with pathway planning aligned to future work or permanent residence options.

Work Permits (LMIA & LMIA-Exempt)

Comprehensive support for employer-specific and open work permits. We assist with LMIA-based applications, LMIA-exempt categories, employer compliance documentation, and work authorization processes.

Investor & Entrepreneur Programs

Guidance for business owners and investors pursuing residency through entrepreneurship streams. Services include program selection, documentation preparation, and business requirements coordination.

Visitor Visas

Support for temporary resident visa applications for tourism, family visits, or business travel. We assist in preparing clear documentation and demonstrating temporary intent to meet current requirements.

Refugee & Humanitarian Applications

Support for individuals seeking protection or permanent residence based on humanitarian and compassionate grounds. Assistance with eligibility review, documentation preparation, and application coordination.

Canadian Citizenship

Guidance for permanent residents applying for Canadian citizenship. Services include eligibility assessment, residency calculation review, application preparation, and documentation support.

Immigration Appeals & Judicial Reviews

Expert representation for immigration appeals and judicial review proceedings. We advocate for your rights and navigate complex legal processes to achieve the best possible outcomes.
